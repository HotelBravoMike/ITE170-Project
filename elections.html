Refer to the table below to see a list of all of the offices up for election in 2019. Descriptions of the office are included.


Offices Up for Election 2019 Prince William County

Office Description
State Delegates (8 within Prince William County) 2-year term. Represent about 80,000 citizens. You vote for 1 delegate.
State Senators (5 within Prince William County) 4-year term. Represent about 200,000 citizens. You vote for 1 senator.
County Supervisors (8 within Prince William County) 4-year term. One supervisor per Magisterial District (7) and a Chairman-at-Large. You vote for one supervisor AND for Chairman.
County School Board Members (8 within Prince William County) 4-year term. One member per Magisterial District (7) and a Chairman-at-large. You vote for one member AND for Chairman.
Soil and Water Directors (3) 4-year term. 3 positions, you vote for each position. Manage conservation of our water and soil within the county.
Commonwealth's Attorney 4-year term. In charge of prosecuting felony crimes in the county.
Sheriff 4-year term. In charge of the Sheriff's Department. Provides security at Judicial Center, serves all court processes and transports prisoners and patients
