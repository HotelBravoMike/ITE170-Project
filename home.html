Do you know who is running this year?

This site aims to educate all voters within Prince William County, Virginia, about which elections are held each year and the candidates running in each race.

This site is non-partisan and non-political. All content shown on candidate pages this website links to is their own intellectual property and opinion. This site is for education, so that you can be an informed voter.

What can we help you with?

  • Click on 2019 Elections to learn which offices are up for election this year.
  • Click on 2019 Candidates to see who is running for each office, and access links to their official campaign websites.
  • Click on Helpful Links to access official websites of Virginia and Prince William County government and other valuable voter information.

Who is Hotel Bravo Mike, anyway? Well, I'm just a regular person who is concerned about the apparent low-level of knowledge around the county about how Virginia elections work. I simply want everyone to know how to make their voices heard at the polls.
